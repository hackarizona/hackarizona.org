Cultivating a world-class Hacker culture in the Southwest.

Hack Arizona is about bringing together the most talented students to represent the burgeoning tech ecosystem that is appearing in the Southwest and across the country.

Hosted at the University of Arizona, participants will build software and hardware projects from start to finish in under 36 hours amongst their peers, mentors and company sponsors.
